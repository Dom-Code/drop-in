How it works

Drop-in is a premium service that will connect you to a highly sought after services. After you make an account you can search through our catalog of participating providers. You will then be asked to provide a refundable deposit before recieving a code and contact information for your provider. You then will contact the provider and set up an appointment. If you are satisfied with the date and time of your appointment, provide the provider with the code.
